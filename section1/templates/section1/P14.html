In the role of person A, you will make a decision for a money allocation. The decision is illustrated below. You can

  • either decide for a money allocation where you receive GHC 8 and person B receives GHC 0 (decision left in the picture)
  • or you decide for an allocation that gives you and person B the same amount x. The "amount x" can be 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8. (decision right in the picture)

You will decide on the minimum amount x where you would prefer "right" to "left".
