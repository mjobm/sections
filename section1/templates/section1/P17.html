Example II:

A person A is asked:

What is the minimum (and equal) amount x where you would switch from the left allocation to the right allocation?

Maximum x: 6.

Payoffs:

The person A decided that the minimum (and equal) amount x where he/she would would switch from left to right is GHC 6. The purple line on the right indicates the switching point at x=6. From there on, person A always prefers equal outcomes over the money allocation on the left.

The computer randomly picks one decision for payment. Let us assume the computer picks decision 5. The payoff for person A is GHC 8 and the payoff for person B is GHC 0 for this decision.
